ASVAB Testing: All juniors and select seniors will be taking the ASVAB career assessment on November 16. This assessment is used to help students determine skills that they have and how these skills fit into selected career choices. The test is also used by the military as their entrance examination, but by no means does taking the test commit students to military service. The information is used by the student and the guidance office to assist in the career and college selection process.

 


Senior Financial Aid Meeting : All seniors and parents are invited to attend an informational meeting on the college financial aid process. Will occur in December. More information to come.


Harrison Central Scholarship Night : All Harrison Central seniors are invited to attend our 6th annual local scholarship night. It will occur near the end of January in the John W. Stephenson Center. More information will be coming.

 

 

Sophomore Visitation to Harrison & Belmont Career Centers : All Harrison Central 10 th graders are invited to attend the trips to Harrison Career Center (Dec. 5 th ), and/or Belmont Career Center (Dec. 12 th ). Permission slips are available in the guidance office.
