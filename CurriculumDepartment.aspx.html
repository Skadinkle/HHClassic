The mission of the Harrison HillsDepartment of Curriculum, Assessment, and Professional Development is toincrease student learning through the development and implementation ofa rigorous and relevant curriculum and the identification of effectiveinstructional resources and strategies that challenge all students tomaximize their potential. 

Collaboration involving a talentedinstructional staff, our number one priority is to provide each studentwith an optimal learning experience that meets his or her needs. 

HHCSD Curriculum Priorities

·          Aligned Curriculum
·          Assessment Maps
·          Differentiated Instruction
·          Step By Step Primary Coaching
·          Guided Inquiry for Math (Everyday Math and Connected Math)
·          ProActive Reading for Reading Intervention
·          Accelerated 7 and 8 Grade Programs
·          New for 08-09 Guided Inquiry Science in the Middle School
